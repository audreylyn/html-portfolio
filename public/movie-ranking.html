The Best Animes according to Audreylyn

My top 3 Animes of all time.

Slam Dank

"Slam Dunk" captures the intensity of basketball, following a protagonist who evolves from an arrogant novice to a dedicated team player.

Hajime No Ippo

"Hajime No Ippo" tells the inspiring story of an underdog who rises in the boxing world, emphasizing hard work and determination.

Tokyo Revengers

"Tokyo Revengers" offers a thrilling mix of time travel and gang conflicts, as its protagonist fights to change the past and save his friends.
